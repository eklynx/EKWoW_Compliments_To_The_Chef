### Basic usage - public

Prerequisites:
- Addon is enabled
- Character is in game with addon enabled.
- No errors are present.
- Current Eating and Well Fed text are set.
- Character is in an public location (such as a major city)

Tests:
1) New eating non-well fed food to finish.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Assure no current active well fed buff is present. Remove if active.
        3) Eat Food that does not provide a well fed benefit until food buff naturally wears off.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - The character should say no other things.


2) New eating with well fed to finish.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Assure no current active well fed buff is present. Remove if active.
        3) Eat Food that provides a well fed benefit until food buff naturally wears off.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - Ten seconds after starting to eat, when the well fed buff appears, the character should print the 'well fed' text in the chat console
        - The character should say no other things.


3) Already well fed, eat non-well fed food to finish.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Assure a well fed buff is active.
        3) Eat Food that does not provide a well fed benefit until food buff naturally wears off.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - The character should say no other things.


4) Already well fed, eat non-well fed food to finish.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Assure a well fed buff is active.
        3) Eat Food that does not provide a well fed benefit until food buff naturally wears off.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - The character should say no other things.


5) Already well fed, eat well fed food to finish.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Assure a well fed buff is active.
        3) Eat Food that provides a well fed benefit until food buff naturally wears off.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - 10 seconds later when the well fed buff refreshes, character should say 'Well fed' text.
        - The character should say no other things.


6) Restart Eating before well fed buff is applied.
    - Steps:
        1) Assure no current active food buff is present. Remove if active.
        2) Eat Food that provides a well fed benefit
        3) Restart eating with a well fed providing food before the well fed buff is applied (before 10 seconds of eating).
        4) Wait until the character finishes eating.
    - Expected Result:
        - Character should print 'Eating' text in the chat console when character starts eating.
        - Character should say 'Well fed' text when the well fed buff is applied 10 seconds after restarting to eat.
        - The character should say no other things.
